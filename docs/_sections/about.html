The Magnetosphere Online Seminar Series is a weekly seminar held every Monday at Noon EST. The Seminar Series will begin with overview talks marching through the magneosphere, interspersed with software, mission, and instrument tutorials. Following these, the Seminar Series will transition to invited and contributed science talks and tutorials.

Click here to join the current Zoom seminar. Note that the seminar requires a password which you can find by joining the Mailing List or in either the SPA or GEM newsletters.

Links to previous talks (and in some cases recording of the talks) can be found under the Speakers and Talks tab.

If you would like to suggest a speaker, seminar topic, or volunteer to present as part of the seminar series please Contact us.

The Seminar Series is jointly hosted by Kyle Murphy (UMD), David Sibeck (NASA GSFC), Homayan Aryan (UCLA), Remya Bhanu (IIG), Sun-Hee Lee (CUA) and Marcos Silveira (CUA). The Magnetosphere Online Seminar Series website is maintained by Kyle Murphy.
