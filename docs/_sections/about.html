The Magnetosphere Online Seminar Series is a weekly seminar held every Monday at Noon EST. The Seminar Series will begin with overview talks marching through the magneosphere, interspersed with software, mission, and instrument tutorials. Following these, the Seminar Series will transition to invited and contributed science talks and tutorials. Click here to join the current seminar. Note that the seminar requires a password which you can find in either the SPA or GEM newsletters

The Seminar Series is jointly hosted by Kyle Murphy (UMD), David Sibeck (NASA GSFC) Homayan Aryan (UCLA), Remya Bhanu (IIG), Sun-Hee Lee (CUA) and Marcos Silveira (CUA). This Magnetosphere Online Seminar Series website is maintained by Kyle Murphy (UMD)
